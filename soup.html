Cream Stew

From The Secret World of Arrietty (2010)

A creamy, chicken and brocolli stew to sip on after 'borrowing' items from humans (If you're a Borrower...)

INGREDIENTS:

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pot and sauté the onions over medium-low heat until they soften.
  2. Add the chicken to the pot and cook until it loses its pink color, ensuring it doesn’t brown.
  3. Toss in the carrots and potatoes, stir-frying for 2 minutes before removing everything from the pot and setting it aside.
  4. Melt butter in the same pot, then stir in the flour to create a roux.
  5. Gradually pour in the chicken stock while stirring to combine.
  6. Return the chicken, mushrooms, and previously set-aside vegetables to the pot. Simmer on low heat, stirring occasionally, until the potatoes are tender, about 15 to 20 minutes.
  7. Add the broccoli along with water, cream, and cream cheese. Cook over low heat for another 10 minutes.
  8. Adjust the seasoning with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Serve the dish hot and enjoy your creamy chicken and vegetable stew!
