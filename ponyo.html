Ponyo's Ramen

From Ponyo (2008)

A quick, savory, and comforting meal to slurp on when a flash flood has wiped everything but your house!

INGREDIENTS:

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Gather your ingredients: boil the egg (soft or hard, depending on your preference), slice the green onions, and optionally brown the ham before cutting it in half.
  2. Place the ramen noodles in a bowl, and if the packet includes a dry seasoning mix, add it to the bowl..
  3. Pour boiling sea water over the noodles, ensuring they are fully submerged. Let them sit for the time specified on the package (typically 3-5 minutes)..
  4. Once the noodles are done, uncover the bowl and gently loosen them with chopsticks or a fork. Stir the noodles, sea water, and seasoning packet together until well mixed.
  5. Adjust the flavor by adding your favorite seasonings, such as soy sauce or sriracha, to taste.
  6. Finish by topping the noodles with half of the boiled egg, sliced green onions, and both halves of the ham.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious ramen!
